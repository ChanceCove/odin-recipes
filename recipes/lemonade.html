Description

Directions to make fresh lemonade

Ingredients
  • 6 to 8 medium sized lemons
  • granulated sugar
  • water
Steps
  • Add sugar and 2 cups of water to a saucepan. Bring to a simmer and stir until sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and cool to room temperature
  • Strain lemon juice into fine mesh sieve into pitcher
  • combine syrup and lemon juice
